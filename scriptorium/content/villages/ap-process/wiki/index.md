# AP Process

The accounts payable processing flow — how invoices enter the system, get captured, get matched, and get paid.

This is actively being designed and the flow will change as the design evolves. The Scriptorium page is the working canvas for that design — every meaningful iteration of the flow gets a fresh demo in `demos/` so the history stays visible.

Existing reports related to AP processing live at `/ap-processing/` on the reports server (e.g. `/ap-processing/status.html`, `/ap-processing/data-handling-notice.html`) and will be migrated into this village's `demos/` over time.

See [[ap-process/flow]] for the current draft of the flow and [[ap-process/integrations]] for what connects to what.
