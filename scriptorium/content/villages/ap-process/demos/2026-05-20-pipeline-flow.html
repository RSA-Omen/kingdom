AP pipeline — invoice flow

An invoice arrives by email and passes through five mechanical stages before either auto-posting or surfacing in the Finance review inbox. The pipeline does no AP policy — it only does the work that can be done without human judgment.

The shape

  • Email → Pronto AP entry is the full happy path, with Finance as the gate before anything posts.
  • Bender's supplier export feeds stage 4 (supplier-match); 3,184 suppliers in the most recent dump.
  • Anthropic's API handles document parsing in stage 3. The data-handling scope is documented in the formal memo demo on this page.
  • Recent benchmark: top invoice ↔ docket matches at 99.2% and 98.9% confidence. Below that threshold, items go to review.
  • The pipeline never guesses AP policy. When confidence is low, Finance sees the candidates and decides.
