Executive Summary

Over the past three years, Gekko has integrated AI-driven tools and automated workflows across the organisation to boost efficiency, spark innovation, and enhance decision-making. Under an Annual Improvement Plan (AIP) dedicated to digital transformation, we have deployed Microsoft 365 Copilot, implemented AI-based workflow automations across IT and finance, and built internal AI knowledge bots — all designed to eliminate manual work and leverage our data assets.

By focusing on awareness, digital literacy, and practical use cases, we have achieved high adoption and tangible benefits. Nearly all employees enabled with Copilot actively use it (≈96%), AI tools now form part of daily workflows, and the value delivered is significant — our Copilot pilot alone yields a conservative 9:1 return on investment. Our internally-built custom tools have logged a further ~166 hours of verified staff time saved since November 2025, across the Interceptor, PDF Page Removal, Gekko Tracks, and the legacy Credit Card Coding system.

We are well positioned to scale these successes. With a dedicated Business Systems Analyst now on the team and strong Board backing, we will continue embedding AI capabilities to drive productivity, innovation, and competitive advantage.

Microsoft 365 Copilot

We introduced Microsoft Copilot — a generative AI assistant embedded in Office 365 — to help employees with writing, analysis, and information retrieval. After a six-person pilot in early 2025, we expanded to 20 users by late 2025, and currently 29 users are licensed. Copilot integration makes AI assistance seamlessly available in Outlook, Word, Excel, Teams, Power BI, and more. User adoption is exceptionally high — ~96% of licensed users actively engage with Copilot — with over 3,600 prompts submitted in the most recent month (23 Apr–22 May 2026). This enthusiastic uptake confirms that embedding AI into routine tools resonates with employees.

The financial case is compelling. Over the six-month pilot (H2 2025), Copilot saved an estimated 500+ staff hours, translating to ~$15,000 in labour value versus ~$3,800 in licence cost — a 3.9× ROI. With expanded usage, recent data is even stronger: 28 active users, ~3,600 prompts, ~180 hours saved (valued at $9,000) at a licence cost of ~$957 — roughly a 9:1 return for the month.

Pronto Knowledge Bot

To support our ERP transformation, we developed an internal Pronto Knowledge Bot — a conversational Q&A system built over thousands of pages of Pronto help documentation. Staff can ask detailed questions about Pronto's features and processes and receive instant answers rather than combing through manuals. The bot has been especially effective for guiding system design; it helped our team configure a CRM for the after-sales service module without external consulting, completing the work at a faster pace. Feedback from staff describes it as "extremely valuable" for understanding the new system. As capabilities expand, the bot is expected to further reduce vendor dependency and accelerate onboarding.

Gekko Tracks — Credit Card Expense Coding

Launched in early 2026, Gekko Tracks is an AI-powered tool for automated credit card expense coding. Machine learning categorises roughly 90% of transactions, requiring only minimal cardholder adjustments. Change management was a key focus — training videos and user guides were distributed before launch, resulting in near-frictionless first-cycle adoption. Two monthly cycles are now complete (February and April 2026), with a third currently underway. The April cycle alone saved ~25 hours: 27 cardholders × 45 min each, plus manager approvals and export time for Finance. Across two completed cycles the running total is ~45 verified hours, with each new cycle expected to add approximately 25 hours.

The Interceptor — Pronto Document Links

The Interceptor is middleware that catches clicks on Pronto's implied document links and automatically redirects staff to the correct NextCloud folder — eliminating manual navigation and wrong-folder errors. 1,072 verified redirects have been logged from system journals since November 2025, saving an estimated ~90 hours of staff time (5 minutes per redirect). April 2026 was the busiest month at 480 redirects; May is tracking at 386 and rising. The tool is used by 16+ staff across Debtors and Purchase-Order workflows. Beyond the time saving, the reliability benefit is significant — before this tool, a broken link in Pronto meant the document was simply not found.

PDF Page Removal Tool

An automated PDF processing tool built for the works-order preparation workflow. Available as both a web interface and an API, it eliminates the need for Acrobat Pro or manual editing. System log analysis revealed 58 confirmed jobs processed since December 2025 across all log files — saving an estimated ~29 hours at 30 minutes per job. Usage is growing: December averaged 2 jobs per week, April averaged 5 jobs per week. All jobs to date are Work Order packs, confirming the tool has become embedded in the procurement workflow.

External Sharing Workflow & Reception Voicemail Routing

Two Power Automate flows delivering reliability and governance benefits. The External Sharing Workflow routes client file-share requests for approval and auto-creates a standardised SharePoint folder — reducing turnaround from hours to minutes and creating a full audit trail. The Voicemail Routing flow ensures every voicemail on the reception line is delivered directly to the right person, eliminating the risk of missed calls that was inherent in a shared-inbox model.

Section 05

Adoption, Awareness & Change Management

Our overarching goal has been to increase digital literacy and effective use of AI tools across Gekko — not just training completion.

🎓

Lunch & Learn Sessions

At least 4 company-wide AI knowledge sessions embedded into Wednesday all-hands meetings since mid-2025. Topics: Copilot prompt best practices, AI ethics, automation quick wins.

📹

Just-in-Time Training

Every major tool deployment is paired with targeted training materials (video guides, user manuals). Gekko Tracks' first cycle had near-zero support requests as a result — staff were ready before go-live.

🏆

AI Champions Network

Early power users of Copilot informally support their colleagues — sharing how they save time day-to-day. Peer-to-peer adoption is faster and more sustainable than top-down mandates.

Our strategy has driven organic usage growth. Rather than measuring training completions, we track actual tool engagement — and every metric points up: Copilot prompts growing month-on-month, Interceptor usage doubling quarter-on-quarter, Gekko Tracks onboarding 17 active users in its first May cycle with 693 field edits already logged. We are still seeking a comprehensive formal AI training curriculum. Until a high-quality, role-specific program is available, our emphasis remains on in-house knowledge sharing and incremental learning — which has proven more effective than generic off-the-shelf courses.

Section 06

Blockers, Dependencies & Resourcing

Challenges encountered and how they were addressed — with lessons applied to future planning.

⚖️

Competing Priorities

AI AIP work competes with major projects (Pronto ERP implementation FY2025, St Ives FY2026). Mid-2025 saw AIP 3.10 marked At Risk due to capacity. Mitigated by adjusting timelines and focusing on quick wins.

👤

Business Systems Analyst Hire

A dedicated BSA was onboarded in August 2025. This single hire directly enabled Gekko Tracks, the Interceptor, and several other workflow improvements — projects that would have stalled without dedicated resource.

📚

Formal Training Gap

Comprehensive AI training tailored to business users has been difficult to source. Available offerings are either too generic or too simplistic. We continue monitoring the market while relying on proven internal methods.

🔌

ERP API Delays

The AP invoice automation was blocked by Pronto Cloud's API not yet being fully available. A workaround was developed that allows testing to proceed independently — project is not blocked and results are promising.

Section 07

Forward Focus & Next Steps

Where we go from here — building on current momentum to scale, formalise, and deepen AI integration.

  • Scale High-Impact Tools: Extend Copilot to more users and explore new use cases (technical teams, project management). Fully deploy AP Invoice Automation by early FY2027, targeting a smooth go-live once testing validates the workaround approach.
  • Formalise AI Training: Roll out a structured AI training program for all staff once a suitable curriculum is identified. This will complement ongoing internal knowledge-sharing and establish a consistent baseline across the company.
  • Refine AI Governance: Finalise and communicate AI acceptable use guidelines — covering responsible usage, data privacy, and the requirement for human oversight. These are currently under internal review.
  • Identify New Opportunities: Through the Strategic Development Group (SDG) and department feedback, continue scanning for AI opportunities — including engineering and R&D applications, and advanced analytics for operations and sales. Priority will be given to initiatives with clear ROI or strategic alignment.
  • Sustain Culture & Momentum: Continue featuring AI success stories in company forums, encourage bottom-up ideas, and publish monthly metrics on hours saved. Gekko's "Smarter Together" culture is a natural fit for AI experimentation — we will harness it to continuously learn and adapt as new capabilities emerge.
Conclusion

Gekko's strategic investment in AI has produced a growing suite of tools and process improvements — from foundational platforms like Copilot to bespoke solutions like Gekko Tracks, the Interceptor, and the Pronto Knowledge Bot. These initiatives are already delivering measurable benefits: saving time, reducing costs, and empowering employees. Together, our custom tools alone have saved ~166 verified hours since November 2025, alongside the ~180 hours per month saved by Copilot. That is real, compounding value that grows with every new cycle, every new redirect, every new document processed.

With strong Board and executive support, a dedicated Business Systems Analyst, and a proven delivery model, we are well placed to scale these successes and explore new frontiers where AI can drive value for Gekko and our stakeholders.
