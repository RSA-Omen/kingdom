Quick Links — user journey

From the user's point of view there are three moments: the Quick Links button in Pronto, the click itself, and the NextCloud document opening. The Interceptor sits in the middle and the user never sees it.

What the user actually sees

  1. The Quick Links button on a Pronto record.
  2. One click — there is no visible loading state because the Interceptor only redirects.
  3. The NextCloud document open in their browser, ready to read.
