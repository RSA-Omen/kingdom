Classify screen — cardholder UI

The screen the cardholder spends almost all their time on. A row per unclassified transaction, ML predictions inline as soft-fill, one-click accept or override per field. Ten rows in, one Submit out.

What's happening on screen

  • Teal rows have a high-confidence ML prediction the cardholder has accepted or can accept with one click.
  • Amber rows have a prediction below the auto-accept threshold (~75%); the cardholder must confirm.
  • Dashed rows have no prediction — the cardholder picks from the dropdown for Project, Cost Category, and GL Account.
  • The Submit button is the only path forward. Submitting flips every row from predicted or unclassified to user_confirmed and notifies the manager.
