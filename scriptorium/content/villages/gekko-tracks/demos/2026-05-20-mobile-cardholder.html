Mobile cardholder — receipt capture & classify

The phone view of Gekko Tracks. Three tabs: Inbox, Ledger, and Receipts. The Receipts tab is the moment-of-purchase tool — tap "Take a photo", the back-facing camera opens, snap, upload. The Inbox is where receipts land after OCR and need a transaction picked.

What this is

  • Three tabs in the bottom nav — Inbox, Ledger, Receipts. Cardholders mostly live on Receipts and Inbox.
  • "Take a photo" opens the back camera directly via the HTML capture="environment" attribute. No native app, no upload-from-gallery step required.
  • Receipt status pills: ✓ matched · ⏳ processing · ▸ needs review.
  • Manual review presents candidate transactions ranked by score; the cardholder picks one with a single tap or reassigns the receipt to someone else.
  • The classify table on desktop collapses into stacked cards on mobile — the same editors, sized for thumb input.
