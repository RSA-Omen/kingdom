Three formats — data & role flow

A transaction enters Gekko Tracks as a single bank-CSV row and leaves as a Pronto-ready export. Three database views progressively enrich it; four roles touch it on the way through.

Notes

  • Format 2 is where the work happens. The Cardholder and the Manager both live in Format 2 — only the scope differs.
  • Format 3 is Finance-only. Tax codes, account references, and CBS values never leak into the cardholder's classify screen.
  • The ML predictor is a participant, not a deciding voice. It speeds the cardholder up; the cardholder still confirms every row.
  • The Pronto handoff is the next big piece. Today, Finance generates a file and uploads it manually; tomorrow [[bender]] will drive AP Entry under Finance's eye.
